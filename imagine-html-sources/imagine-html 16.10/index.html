Imagine All the People

We are an online community of people that believe in John Lennon's dream of a peaceful world without possessions, countries, and religions. We are starting a project to make this dream real. We start with ourselves and share our small ideas and complete small actions daily. We just want to start the process of changing possessions, countries and religions little by little.

If one day (2.222) all possessions, religions and countries are being shared (bottom up) in the end these 3 big powers that nowadays cause so many problems will be eliminated...

Sharing our books with the neighboors

My wife and I love reading and in the past few years we accumulated a lot of books worth sharing with other people. We don't want to get rid of our books, we want to share them! That's why we decided to create our micro library for our neighbours so that more people can enjoy our books. Perhaps our single action of changing private possession into shared possession will not change the world, but what if one day thousands or millions of persons will decide to do the same?

Promoting and sharing Auroville in India

Inspired by the "Idea in Action" at this platform that is promoting The Venus Project I thought that is was also a good idea to let the people know about the Auroville project, www.auroville.org Auroville is a universal town in India that was founded in 1968 (!) and it is an incredible example of how people of 50 different nations really can live together in peace without possessions, religions and countries. This news should have the same or even more attention in the press as for example a Barça-Madrid soccer match. But as this is not the case (unfortunately...) I decided to start sharing this project and will not stop doing so untill everybody sees with her or his own eyes that an Imagine world is really possible. So if you join my group let's work together on a kind of global communication plan for Auroville so that every single person (starting at schools with young children) will know about it...
